IAS, IPS, IFS (Foreign) + 20+ Group A & B All-India & Central Services

Any recognised University Degree
Age Limit
21–30 (SC/ST +5, OBC +3)
Attempts
4 (OBC: 7, SC/ST: unlimited)

Indian citizen (IAS/IPS); broader for others

Written + Interview for final service allocation

All India: IAS, IPS, IFS (Foreign)
Group A: Revenue, Postal, Audit & Accounts, Railway (multiple), Customs, CBI–DSP, CISF-AC, Defence Estate, Information, Trade
Group B: Central Secretariat, Railway Board Secretariat, AFHQ, Customs Appraisers, DANICS, Pondicherry Civil Services
